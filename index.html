I'm an independent digital marketing consultant with over 20 years of experience across in-house marketing roles, agency-side strategy, and long-term freelance engagements. Previous in-house experience includes Edrington Group and Energy Saving Trust; current clients are primarily UK-based SMEs and established brands.

My methodology is straightforward: audit what exists, fix the fundamentals, then scale what the data supports. I don't recommend channels for their own sake — only where the commercial case is clear and measurable.

Clients come to me when they need someone who sees across SEO, paid search, paid social, and analytics simultaneously — and who can give a straight answer on where their budget is best placed.

How I work

Audit. Fix. Scale.

Step 01

Audit & diagnose

Every engagement starts with an honest assessment — traffic, spend, attribution, and conversion. No assumptions, no generic recommendations.

Step 02

Fix the foundations

Tracking gaps, structural account issues, and technical blockers addressed first. Scaling broken campaigns wastes budget faster.

Step 03

Build & test

Campaigns, content, and dashboards built to a clear brief. Ongoing testing with a bias for commercial outcomes — ROAS, CAC, and CLV over vanity metrics.

Step 04

Report & iterate

Clear reporting against agreed KPIs. Strategy adapts as the data evolves. What's working, what isn't, and what's next — every month.
