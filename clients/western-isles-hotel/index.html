Western Isles Hotel — search and paid media for a Scottish island hotel

Western Isles Hotel is an iconic hotel overlooking Tobermory Bay on the Isle of Mull. Digital marketing work covered SEO, Google Ads, Meta Ads, and local search visibility for the hotel and its restaurant.

Overview

Driving direct bookings for a destination hotel in a competitive travel market

Services delivered

SEO Google Ads Meta Ads Local SEO Content Schema Seasonal Campaign Planning

The Western Isles Hotel sits in one of Scotland's most photographed locations — Tobermory Bay — but competes against OTA platforms and destination travel sites with significantly larger budgets. The goal was to grow direct bookings and reduce reliance on commission-heavy channels.

SEO focused on destination and accommodation terms for Mull and Tobermory, with local business schema and content around the hotel's unique position. Google Ads covered branded defence and non-branded accommodation terms with seasonal campaign scheduling.

Meta Ads targeted both lookalike audiences based on past guests and interest-based prospecting targeting Scottish island travel and whisky tourism audiences.
