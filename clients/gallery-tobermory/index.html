Gallery Tobermory — ecommerce and local search for a Scottish art gallery

Gallery Tobermory is an art gallery and online retailer based in Tobermory, Isle of Mull, selling original artwork and prints. Work covered SEO, Google Ads, and ecommerce strategy for their online shop.

Overview

Bridging gallery footfall and online art sales

Services delivered

SEO Google Ads Google Shopping Ecommerce Strategy Content Product Schema

Gallery Tobermory operates as both a physical destination gallery and an ecommerce store — two audiences with different intent. Visitors to Tobermory represent impulse purchasers, while online buyers are typically seeking specific Scottish art or artist-led work.

SEO strategy separated local gallery intent from ecommerce product terms, with optimised product listings and artist-level content to capture long-tail searches. Google Ads covered Shopping campaigns for prints and originals, and branded search protection.

The ecommerce strategy focused on improving average order value through product bundling recommendations and improving the buyer journey from artist page to checkout.
