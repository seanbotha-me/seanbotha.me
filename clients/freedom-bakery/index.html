Freedom Bakery — brand voice and social strategy for a social enterprise

Freedom Bakery is a Scottish social enterprise providing employment and training to people affected by the justice system. Work covered paid social, organic social content, tone of voice development, and recruitment marketing.

Overview

Building brand awareness and purpose-led content for a social enterprise

Services delivered

Paid Social Organic Social Content Strategy Tone of Voice Recruitment Marketing Meta Ads

Freedom Bakery's mission — reducing reoffending through meaningful employment — is both their greatest differentiator and their biggest communication challenge. Content has to be authentic, human, and commercially compelling without being exploitative or preachy.

Work included developing a content tone and voice framework aligned to the brand's values, and building an organic social content calendar that mixed product storytelling with human impact. Paid social amplified best-performing content and supported wholesale B2B lead generation.

Recruitment marketing was a specific workstream — attracting both commercial bakers and individuals from the justice-involved community, two very different audiences requiring separate messaging and channel strategies.
